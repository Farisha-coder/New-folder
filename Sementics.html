facts about Birds

Birds are warm-blooded, feathered vertebrates belonging to the class Aves.Birds are fascinating creatures that belong to the class Aves and are known for their feathers, beaks, and ability to fly. They inhabit diverse environments, from dense forests to open oceans, and come in a wide range of sizes and colors. While most birds can fly, some, like ostriches and penguins, have adapted to life on land or in water. Birds play important roles in ecosystems, such as controlling insect populations, pollinating plants, and dispersing seeds. Many species migrate across vast distances to find food and suitable breeding grounds. With their incredible diversity and unique adaptations, birds continue to captivate scientists and nature enthusiasts alike.
