A bookfair is a joyful fair where are displayed and sold to the common people.Today a book fair has become very popular in our country.It is held in some special occasions is towns and cities every year.The bangla academy organizes a month long book fair on the occasion of the International mother`s language dayon it`s compound.Besides,the famous book publishers also organize book fair sometimes.

A book fair usually last for a week or even a month.Hundreds of book publishers take part in the Ekushe book fair.The stalls sits in rows.They are decorated nicely.Books on various branches both in bangla and english are displayed and sold here.

The book lover and reader gather in a book fair.It is large gathering of teachers,studants and educated people,sometimes sccholars,writes and poets also visit a book fair.Seminar on books in also held here.

A book fair is an important part of our education.It helps to broaden of our kingdom of knowledge. Books are sold at a cheap rate in a book fair.A book fair forms the habit of reading,buying,writing and taking care of books.Every students and every educated person should visit the book fair every year.
